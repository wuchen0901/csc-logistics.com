About Us

Convoy Supply Chain Limited is a young dynamic company, specializing in managing international freight forwarding processes.

Convoy Supply Chain Limited

The founders understood their client's needs with regard to forwarding and related services.As experts in both supply chain management and logistics, we have developed a unique methodology for managing the processes of the worldwide freight forwarding.

Convoy provides and manages supply chain solutions for thousands of companies every year – from the small family run business to the large corporation. We work passionately to deliver great customer experiences and high-quality services – and help ensure a steady supply of goods to production lines, outlets, stores and consumers all over the world.
