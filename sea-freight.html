Is price your main thought?

Convoy's sea freight services are your best choice when price is one of your main criteria. Sea freight is the slowest method of transport, but if time is not a factor for you then a ship wins every time. And of course, some goods simply cannot go by air due to their size or weight or the fact that they are not permitted on board an aircraft.

With the enormous volumes of freight that we ship and the multitude of carriers we work with, we can offer you competitive rates, frequent departures and the capacity you need - from and to every major port in the world.
